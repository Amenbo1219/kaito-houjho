# Kaito Houjho
Kaito Houjho is an independent researcher. He received his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science from Tokyo University of Technology in 2024 and 2026, respectively. Until 2026, he was a member of the Computer Vision Laboratory at the same university. His research interests include computer vision, with a particular focus on image geometry and free-viewpoint image synthesis.
## Professional Background & Skills
He also possesses extensive knowledge of server administration and Public Address (PA) systems. Regarding his organizational background,　he was a member of [Advanced Creators](https://advancedcreators.undo.jp/) until his departure in 2024.  Subsequently, he founded Ajara and has served as its primary organizer since 2024.
Currently, he is active in a wide range of fields, including the provision of [Server Management Tools](https://github.com/Amenbo1219) and various technical solutions.
